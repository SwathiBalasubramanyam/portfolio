Recent Projects

ChatLog

ChatLog is a full stack clone of the popular business messaging application, Slack. ChatLog is a workspace communication tool that allows users to collaborate and connect with one another through a variety of ways. Inspired by slack, the core feature of ChatLog is the ability to send messages to another user or to a channel they have access to.

Rocky Road Trip

Rocky Road Trip aims at eliminating cumbersome spreadsheets, endless lists and conflicting suggestions that come when planning a road trip. Rocky Road Trip helps you craft your ideal road trip with just a few clicks. From must see cities to hidden gems, we help you chart the ultimate route tailored to your tastes. I was the backend lead for this project and built it along with two other engineers in a weeks time.

JobTrends

Job Trends is a data visualization site that fetches and displays job postings for a given title. Job Trends, aggregates all the skills, companies hiring, job posting locations from job posts and displays as pie charts, representing a quick snapshot of what the job postings contain.
